General Handyman / Maintenance Technician

Apply Now

Position Summary

We're looking for a skilled, self-sufficient General Handyman to handle a wide range of light commercial/industrial maintenance and renovation work. This role is ideal for someone who's genuinely handy — capable of moving between plumbing, electrical, carpentry, and finish work without needing "hand-holding." You'll be the go-to person for facility upkeep, improvement work, and renovation projects.

Key Skills below is a general list — expectations may vary based on project scope.

Plumbing

  • Swap out toilets, flush valves, and fixtures in commercial/industrial settings
  • Rough-in plumbing for new fixtures or remodels
  • Replace faucets, sinks, and supply lines
  • Basic diagnosis of leaks, clogs, and pressure issues

Electrical

  • Replace outlets, switches, and light fixtures
  • Troubleshoot simple circuit issues (breakers, dead outlets)
  • Install ceiling fans, exterior lighting, and interior fixtures
  • Know when to flag work that requires a licensed electrician

Carpentry & General Construction

  • Light framing, trim, and door/window repair or replacement
  • Drywall installation, patching, taping, and finishing
  • Interior and exterior painting (prep, priming, finish coats)
  • General building renovation, additions, remodels, buildouts

Flooring

  • Tile installation and repair (setting, grouting, layout)
  • LVP (luxury vinyl plank) installation
  • Epoxy flooring application (coating prep, application, curing)
  • Subfloor repair/leveling as needed

Exterior / Site Work

  • Chain-link fence installation and repair (posts, tension wire, fabric, gates)
  • General site maintenance and small structural repairs

General

  • Diagnose problems independently and determine the right fix
  • Source materials and communicate needs to ownership/ops
  • Maintain a clean, safe job site
  • Complete work orders and communicate status/completion clearly

Qualifications

  • 3+ years of hands-on experience across multiple trades (plumbing, electrical, carpentry, flooring)
  • Working knowledge of basic code requirements (even if not licensed)
  • Valid driver's license & reliable transportation
  • Comfortable working independently with minimal supervision
  • Physically able to lift 50+ lbs, work on ladders, kneel/crouch for extended periods
  • OSHA 10 a plus, not required
